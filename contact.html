Frequently Asked Questions

What is artificial silver?

+

Artificial silver is a high-quality metal alloy that closely resembles real silver in appearance but is more affordable. It's durable, tarnish-resistant, and perfect for everyday wear.

How do I care for my jewelry?

+

To keep your jewelry looking its best, store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Clean it with a soft cloth and mild soap when needed. Avoid exposing it to harsh chemicals or perfumes.

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original packaging. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you offer international shipping?

+

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. You can view shipping options at checkout.
